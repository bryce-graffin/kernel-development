## Books
### The C Language
1. *The C Programming Language* - Kernighan & Ritchie (K&R)
- considered the "gold standard"
- can be paired well with practice on small programs before diving deeper

### OS Fundamentals
1. *Operating Systems: Three Easy Pieces (OSTEP)* - Remzi & Andrea Arpaci-Dusseau
* available online at ostep.org
* widely loved for being approachable w/out being shallow

### Linux Kernel
1. *Linux Kernel Development* - Robert Love
* great bridge into the actual kernel codebase
* practical, readable, give a real sense of kernel organization

2. *Understanding the Linux Kernel* - Bovet & Cesati
* goes deeper than Love
* more detailed
